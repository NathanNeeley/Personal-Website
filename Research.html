Research




At this point, my research has considered Stravinsky's serial music, Ives's diatonic and chromatic music, and songs from Disney movies through the lens of a multiplicity of methodologies. For future research, I hope to continue my research into Disney films to observe fascinating musical relationships and how they contribute to the plot's narrative. I also plan to continue researching Ives's music to better understand the structure of his diatonic and chromatic music, as well as his microtonal music. Provided below is a list of my polished research projects (conference papers, etc.) in chronological order from recent to oldest, with handouts, abstracts, and/or full papers included for select items.
