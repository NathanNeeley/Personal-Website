Research




At this point, the majority of my research has considered Stravinsky's serial music through the lens of a multiplicity of methodologies, elucidating interesting aspects of his music. A secondary focus has involved film music, particularly analyzing Disney songs, recoinnering them in a music-theoretic context. Provided below is a list of my polished research projects (conference papers, etc.) in chronological order from current to oldest, with handouts, abstracts, and/or full papers included for select items.
